SQUEALERS | Rock 'n' Roll band

Squeal­ers is a rock ’n’ roll band from Jy­väs­ky­­lä, Finland. Our focus is on writing tight, riff-driven rock music.

Heavily influenced by AC/DC, we mix in covers with original compositions in our live show.

Stream our original music and follow us for the latest shows and updates.

Background

Squealers was founded in 2017, originally as a loosely defined two-man tribute to AC/DC. Founding members Tuukka Pitkänen and Veikko Hoffman released the band’s first EP, Rock Your Balls Out, in 2021. Consisting of three AC/DC covers and one original composition, this project was followed by the Big Dog EP (2023), taking the direction of original rock ’n’ roll music inspired by AC/DC.

In 2024, Squealers evolved from a duo into a full-fledged band, with the addition of members Jukka, Taneli and Antti. As of 2025, the band has played a handful of gigs in Jyväskylä, with the set lists combining AC/DC covers and Squealers originals. An album of original music is in the works, so it’s safe to say that the full story of Squealers is only beginning.
